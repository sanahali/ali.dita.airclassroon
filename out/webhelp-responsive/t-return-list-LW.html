Rental Return Checklist

Below you'll find a quick checklist to ensure that you aren't missing anything that will cause a late fee on your rental upon return.

  1. Charge the product to 100%.

  2. Make sure all chargers and the rented product are brought back in the Virginia Tech case that they came in.

  3. Bring a credit card or cash to pay your rental fee if you have not already paid online.

If any of these steps are incomplete, you will be charged a $25 late fee.
