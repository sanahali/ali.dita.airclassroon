    How to use the Mac Desktop Computer

    The Mac Desktops are top of the line computers that are up to date with the latest Apple technology.

    In order to login to the Mac Desktop you'll need your username and 6-digit password that were sent to you by AirClassroom when you booked the space. Look for an email entitled "AirClassroom Shanks 360 Guest".
    1. Power on computer by holding down the "home" button for 5 seconds.
    2. Enter your username and password, which were sent to you by AirClassroom.
    3. Follow the steps for Two-Factor Authentication to access the WiFi (see Two-Factor Authentication tab for how to use this feature).
    Be sure to save all of your information online via Google Drive or on a flash drive to ensure you do not lost any information. The computers are reset at the end of each day.
