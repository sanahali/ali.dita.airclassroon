How to Clean Up Shanks 360

We are committed to keeping our spaces clean for our guests. Pay it forward and make sure that you are leaving the room as clean as you found it.

Sometimes when you are picking up a room it is easy to overlook the smaller details. We have detailed a step-by-step process for a final check on the room before you head out:

  1. Walk through the rows of desks with the trash can in hand and throw out any trash you see.
  2. Do a similar walk through again, now looking at the floor to ensure there are no chargers that were left plugged in, or trash on the floor.
  3. Take cleaning wipes from the cabinet and wipe over the desk surfaces and the desktop monitors. As you do this, make sure the monitors are turned off.
  4. Erase any remaining writing/drawing from the white board.
  5. Take the trash bag out of the bin and carry it out with you to the dumpster.
  6. Turn off all lights before closing the door behind you.
